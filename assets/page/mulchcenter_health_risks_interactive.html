Key Findings at a Glance

Official Classification

Group 1

Wood dust is classified as a "known human carcinogen" by the IARC and the U.S. NTP.

Primary Cancer Risk

Sino-nasal Cancers

The strongest and most consistent evidence links wood dust exposure to cancers of the nasal cavity and paranasal sinuses.

Beyond Cancer

Respiratory Issues

Exposure can also lead to non-cancerous health problems including asthma, bronchitis, dermatitis, and allergic reactions.
